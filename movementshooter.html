Project Summary

Movement Shooter was my final project for my CG Spectrum course. This project was used to show off everything that I had learned as well as to show that I had the capacity to teach myself new techniques and be able to move on whenever I got stuck on a problem.

Project Features
Shooting Mechanics

The shooting mechanics in this game are made up of actually shooting the weapon, Zooming in and Aiming, Reloading the Weapon, and Managing it's recoil.

Movement

The Player character can Double Jump, Dash, and Crouch.
